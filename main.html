Baghdad Shareef


A major center of Islamic history, the city is known for its numerous historic mosques. It includes museums such as the Iraq Museum, Baghdadi Museum and Abd al-Karim Qasim Museum. Baghdad is also nicknamed as "City of Palaces", as its home to numerous palaces such as Abbasid Palace, Radwaniyah Palace and Al-Faw Palace.
